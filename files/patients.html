📝 Table Summary:

💡 The table is mainly about: Person

Details: The table 'patients' captures comprehensive demographic and personal information about individuals receiving medical care. It begins with identifiers such as Id, BIRTHDATE, DEATHDATE, and SSN (Social Security Number), which are crucial for distinguishing each patient and tracking their lifespan and identity. Driving and international travel capabilities are indicated by DRIVERS_LICENSE and PASSPORT. Personal details include names and titles, represented by NAME_PREFIX, FIRST_NAME, LAST_NAME, NAME_SUFFIX, and for those who may have changed their names, MAIDEN_NAME. Social and demographic data are reflected in attributes like MARITAL_STATUS, RACE, ETHNICITY, and GENDER. The BIRTHPLACE attribute provides the location of birth, while ADDRESS, CITY, STATE, COUNTY, FIPS_CODE, and ZIP_CODE offer a detailed residential address. Geographical coordinates are stored in LATITUDE and LONGITUDE. Financial aspects are covered by HEALTHCARE_EXPENSES and HEALTHCARE_COVERAGE, indicating the costs associated with healthcare and the extent of insurance coverage, respectively. Lastly, INCOME provides economic status information. Overall, the table presents a holistic view of patients' personal and demographic profiles, along with their healthcare-related financial data.
