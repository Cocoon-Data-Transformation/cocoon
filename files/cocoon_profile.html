Table Summary

The table is about individuals' personal and demographic information, along with their healthcare and financial details. Each person has a unique Id. Their life events are marked by BIRTHDATE and possibly DEATHDATE. Identifying numbers include SSN, DRIVERS, and PASSPORT. Names are broken down into PREFIX, FIRST, LAST, SUFFIX, and MAIDEN. MARITAL status, RACE, ETHNICITY, and GENDER describe personal characteristics. BIRTHPLACE tells where they were born, while ADDRESS, CITY, STATE, COUNTY, FIPS, ZIP, LAT, and LON provide current living location details. Financial aspects are covered by HEALTHCARE_EXPENSES, HEALTHCARE_COVERAGE, and INCOME.
