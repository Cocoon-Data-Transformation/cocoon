📝 Table Summary:

💡 The table is mainly about: Sales Transaction

Details: The table captures details of Sales Transaction data, encompassing a total of 34867 records, each with 15 attributes that describe the specifics of each sale. The Date attribute indicates when the transaction occurred, while the Year and Month attributes provide a temporal breakdown of the sales data. The Customer Age and Customer Gender attributes offer demographic insights into the purchasers. Geographical data is represented by the Country and StateOfSale attributes, which specify where the sales took place. The nature of the products sold is detailed by the Product Category and ProductSubCategory attributes, which classify the items into broader and more specific groups, respectively. The Quantity of products sold in each transaction is recorded, along with the Unit Cost and Unit Price, which provide financial details about the cost to the seller and the price to the buyer. The financial impact of each sale is further detailed by the TotalCost and Revenue attributes, which represent the aggregate cost and income from the sale. Lastly, the AdditionalData attribute is available for any supplementary information that does not fit within the other attributes.
