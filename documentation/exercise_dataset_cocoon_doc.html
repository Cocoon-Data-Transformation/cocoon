📝 Table Summary:

💡 The table is mainly about: Physical Activity or Exercise

Details: The table 'exercise_dataset' provides detailed information on the caloric expenditure associated with various physical activities or exercises. At its core, the table lists different types of activities under the Activity, Exercise or Sport (1 hour) attribute, indicating the type and intensity of exercise performed for one hour. For individuals of varying weights, the table specifies the estimated number of calories burned, which is categorized under four distinct attributes: CaloriesBurned_130lb, CaloriesBurned_155lb, CaloriesBurned_180lb, and CaloriesBurned_205lb. These attributes represent the caloric burn for individuals weighing 130, 155, 180, and 205 pounds, respectively. Additionally, the table includes the CaloriesBurned_PerKg attribute, which provides a metric measurement of calories burned per kilogram of body weight, allowing for a more universal application of the data across different weight measurements. Together, these attributes offer a comprehensive view of the energy expenditure for a variety of physical activities, tailored to individuals of different body weights.
