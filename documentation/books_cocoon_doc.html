💡 The table is mainly about: Book

Details: The table provides a comprehensive dataset about books, encompassing a variety of attributes that describe each book in detail. The dataset contains 6810 rows, indicating that there are 6810 individual books cataloged, and 12 columns, each representing a specific attribute of the books. The isbn13 and isbn10 are unique identifiers for each book, serving as a standardized international code and a shorter national code, respectively. The title attribute gives the name of the book, while the subtitle provides additional information about the title if applicable. The authors attribute lists the individuals who wrote the book, and the categories attribute classifies the book into genres or types, such as "Fiction" or "Detective and mystery stories". Visual representation of the book is found in the thumbnail_url, which links to an image of the book's cover. The description attribute offers a synopsis or a brief overview of the book's content, giving potential readers insight into the narrative or subject matter. The published_year attribute indicates the year the book was released, providing a temporal context for the work. Reader reception and book length are represented by average_rating, num_pages, and ratings_count. The average_rating is a numerical value reflecting the aggregated opinions of readers, while num_pages reveals the book's length, and ratings_count shows the number of times the book has been rated, which can be an indicator of its popularity or readership.
