Table Summary:

The table is mainly about: Divorce Case

Details: The table 'divorces_2000_2015_original' provides detailed records of divorce cases from the years 2000 to 2015. It captures a comprehensive set of attributes related to both spouses involved in each case, as well as information about the marriage and divorce proceedings. At the core of the table is the Fecha de Registro, which indicates when the divorce case was officially recorded. The type of divorce is specified under TipoDivorcio, which can be either 'Necesario' or 'Voluntario', reflecting whether the divorce was contested or by mutual agreement. Each spouse's personal details are extensively documented. For the husband, attributes include NacionalidadEsposo, FechaNacimientoEsposo, CiudadNacimientoEsposo, MunicipioNacimientoEsposo, EstadoNacimientoEsposo, PaisNacimientoEsposo, EdadEsposo, MunicipioResidenciaEsposo, EstadoResidenciaEsposo, PaisResidenciaEsposo, IngresoEsposo, OcupacionEsposo, and CiudadResidenciaEsposo. These attributes cover his nationality, date and place of birth, age, place of residence, income, and occupation. Similarly, the wife's information is captured through NacionalidadEsposa, FechaNacimientoEsposa, FechaRegistroEsposa, CiudadNacimientoEsposa, MunicipioNacimientoEsposa, EstadoNacimientoEsposa, PaisNacimientoEsposa, EdadEsposa, CiudadResidenciaEsposa, MunicipioResidenciaEsposa, EstadoResidenciaEsposa, PaisResidenciaEsposa, OcupacionEsposa, and IngresoEsposa. The marriage itself is represented by the FechaMatrimonio, indicating when the couple got married. The divorce details are provided under LocalidadDivorcio, MunicipioDivorcio, and EstadoDivorcio, which describe where the divorce took place. Educational and employment backgrounds for both spouses are also included, with EscolaridadEsposo and EscolaridadEsposa detailing their respective levels of education, and TrabajoEsposo and TrabajoEsposa describing their employment status. The duration of the marriage is reflected in AnosCasados and MesesCasados, providing insight into how long the couple was married before divorcing. The number of children resulting from the marriage is noted in Num_Hijos, and the Custodia attribute indicates who was granted custody of the children post-divorce. In summary, this table offers a holistic view of divorce cases, encompassing demographic, socioeconomic, and marital details of the individuals involved, as well as specifics about the divorce itself.
