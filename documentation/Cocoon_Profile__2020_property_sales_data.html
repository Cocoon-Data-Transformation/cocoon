Table Summary

The table contains information about Residential property sales. Each property has a unique PropertyID, taxkey, and Address. The properties are located in District 9 and nbhd 40. They have various Styles such as Colonial, Ranch, and Cape Cod, with different Extwall materials. The properties have different Stories, Year_Built, number of Rooms, FinishedSqft, Units, Bdrms, Fbath, Hbath, and Lotsize. The Sale_date and Sale_price are recorded for each property sale.
