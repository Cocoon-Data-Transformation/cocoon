Table Summary

The table is about records of individuals with details about their personal and demographic information. Each entry includes the Fecha_de_Registro which tells us when the record was made. The Tipo attribute indicates the type of record. People's Nacionalidad is listed, showing they are mostly Mexican. Their birth details are captured in F_Naci_Do, LocNaci_Do, MpioNaci_Do, EFedNaci_Do, and PaisNaci_Do. The Edad attribute shows their age. Where they live is described by MpioHab_Do, EFedHab_Do, and PaisHab_Do. Their income is noted in ElIngreso, and what they do for work is in Ocupaci__n_Do. The LocHabi_Do and Nacionalidad2 attributes provide more about their location and nationality. There are similar attributes for another person in the record, starting with F_Naci_Da and ending with Ocupaci__n_Da, which mirror the first set of attributes but for a second individual. The EllaIngreso attribute shows this second person's income. The table also includes the date of a specific event in Fecha, and where it happened in Localidad, Municipio, and Entidad_Federativa. Education and employment details for both individuals are in ElEscolaridad, ElTrabajo, EllaEscolaridad, and EllaTrabajo. The duration of a marriage is noted in Matri_A__os and Matri_Meses, and the number of children in Num_Hijos. Finally, who has the Custodia of the children is also mentioned.
