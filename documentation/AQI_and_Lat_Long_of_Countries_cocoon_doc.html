Table Summary:

The table is mainly about: Air Quality Measurement by City

Details: The table 'AQI_and_Lat_Long_of_Countries' provides detailed air quality measurements for various cities across different countries. It encompasses a total of 16695 rows, each representing a unique set of data points, and includes 14 distinct attributes that contribute to a comprehensive understanding of the air quality in each location. The Country and City attributes specify the geographical location where the air quality data is collected. The AQI Value and AQI Category attributes give a general assessment of the overall air quality index and its corresponding health implications. Specific pollutants are also individually assessed, with CO AQI Value and CO AQI Category providing carbon monoxide levels and their health categories, Ozone AQI Value and Ozone AQI Category detailing ozone concentrations, NO2 AQI Value and NO2 AQI Category reporting on nitrogen dioxide levels, and PM2.5 AQI Value and PM2.5 AQI Category focusing on fine particulate matter measurements. Lastly, the Latitude and Longitude attributes offer precise geographical coordinates for each city, allowing for location-based analysis and mapping of the air quality data.
